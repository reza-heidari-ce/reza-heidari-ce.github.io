Bio

I am a Computer Engineering B.Sc student at Sharif University of Technology. I have worked on a robust medical classification project under Prof. Mitra at L3S Research Center. I have also had the privilege of working with Prof. Han and Prof. Mitra on a Medical Visual Question Answering in collaboration with L3S Research Center, The University of Sydney, and The University of Melbourne. Last summer I worked on scene generation from scene graphs under Prof. Tombari and Prof. Navab.

My current research interests lie in Medical Informatics and Computer Science for Healthcare .
